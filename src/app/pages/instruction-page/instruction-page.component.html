~ The Crystal Hunt ~

How to play?
You will use your keyboard to move the knight. You will get score and energy by killing the monsters. Destroy all the crystal will end the round and proceed to the next one.

Control
[UP ARROW] Jump, [DOWN ARROW] Dodge
[LEFT ARROW] Move left, [RIGHT ARROW] Move right
[A] Slash attack, [S] Charge attack
[D] Shield, [SPACE] Blast attack
